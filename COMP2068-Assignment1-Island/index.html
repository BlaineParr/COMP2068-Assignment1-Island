HEAD into the jungle.

SWIM towards the islands.

You head into the jungle, but you are quite thirsty. You see a tree with coconuts in it, but you also see what looks like it could be a body of fresh water closer to the centre of the island.

CLIMB the tree.

HEAD towards the water.

You attempt to climb the tree. As you are climbing you lose your grip and fall head-first to the ground. You died.

You head to the water you spotted. As you approach, you notice a strange altar infront of the lake. You also notice something shining off to your left.

Head to the ALTAR.

Search for the source of the SHINING.

As you approach the altar a large tentacle reaches out of the water and grabs your leg. Before you have a chance to act you are dragged under water. The last thing you see is a large open mouth. You died.

You run at the Kraken ready to punch with all your might. Before you can, the Kraken whips out a tentacle tripping you. The Kraken then wraps one of its tentacles around you and throws you into its mouth. You died.

You turn and run faster than you knew you could, however it's no use. In a swift movement the Kraken raises one of its tentacles and slams it down on you. You died.

You head towards the area you saw the shining light. You find a sword with the tip of its blade stuck into the ground. You take the sword. You could now go to the altar you saw earlier or head back to where you awoke.

GO to the altar.

BACK to where you awoke.

You swim towards the islands. Halfway through your swim, you must decide if you want to head to the left island, or the right island.

Swim to the LEFT island.

Swim to the RIGHT island.

As you swim towards the left island, you find a strange wooden idol floating in the ocean. It looks like a squid. You feel like the idol is somehow related to the island you just left. You take the idol. Should you continue swimming to the left island, instead go to the right island or go back to where you came from?

Continue to the LEFT island.

Swim to the RIGHT island.

Go BACK.

As you get close to the island the idol you're carrying begins to glow. The water around you starts to swirl. The swirling becomes faster and faster. A whirlpool has formed around you! You are sucked underwater and as you submerge the idol escapes your hand. The violent thrashing of the water whips you around -- conciousness fades. You died.

As you swim towards the right island, you see a shark in your path. He is swimming directly towards you... he sees you too! You have two choices, attempt to fight the shark or swim away as fast as you can.

FIGHT the shark.

SWIM away.

You punch the shark on the nose as hard as you can. He doesn't flinch. The shark proceeds to bite you. You died.

You swim as fast as you can back towards the island you came from, but it's no use. The shark is too fast and catches up to you. You feel the shark bite your legs and you lose conciousness. You died.
